    About Us

    ThermoCal, Inc. was founded in 2021 with the mission of providing customer-oriented solutions that maintain the optimum operation of your current HVAC and Refrigeration system to maximize the lifespan and operational reliability of the asset while keeping operating costs at a minimum through a proactive approach to service and maintenance.

    Our Mission

    Building an HVAC and Refrigeration company with cloud infrastructure to reduce overhead, provide higher wages to our team, offer lower costs to you, with advanced diagnostics and cost-related data of your heating and cooling system.

    Our Plan

    To utilize modern technology to reduce overhead and provide innovative solutions that uphold the operation of your current system and deliver you with cost-minded options for service maintenance, repair, and new equipment sales.

    Future Vision

    Create a company culture with a decentralized infrastructure with a sustainable business model that puts purpose and passion for the industry over profit. The intent is to provide a purpose-driven company for the community we serve.

    Why choose ThermoCal

    ThermoCal, Inc. supports small businesses and goverment with modern HVAC/R solutions for your building's heating and air system. Join us on the road to green technology and a world where cooling your business doesn't cost you your planet. With a specialization in commercial HVAC and Refrigeration, our customer care and the quality of your air are our priorities. You can rely on us to support you in making educated decisions on the best-case solution.
